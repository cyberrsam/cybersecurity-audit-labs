## Overview
This project simulates an internal IT audit for a fictional company, Botium Toys, using the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF).

## Audit Objectives
- Assess security posture
- Identify risks to critical assets
- Evaluate PCI-DSS and GDPR compliance
- Provide prioritized recommendations

## Framework Used
- NIST Cybersecurity Framework (Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover)

## Summary of Findings
- Lack of Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
- Weak password controls
- Limited monitoring and logging
- No formal incident response plan
- GDPR compliance gaps

## Overall Risk Posture
Moderate to High risk due to authentication weaknesses and compliance exposure.
